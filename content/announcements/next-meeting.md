<h2 style="text-align: center;">Launch of the Australian Lipid Society</h2>

The Society's next meeting is the **Australian Lipid Meeting 7 (ALM7)** held jointly with the **5th International Lipidomics Society (iLS) Conference**, taking place **18–21 October 2026** at the Novotel Perth Langley, Perth, Western Australia.

For full details - program, speakers, registration, and travel awards - see the [Events page](/events/).
