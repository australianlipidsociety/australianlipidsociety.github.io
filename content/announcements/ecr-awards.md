We are pleased to announce that MDPI's *Metabolites* and *Nutrients* journals will sponsor early-career researcher (ECR) awards at the **Australian Lipid Meeting 7 & 5th International Lipidomics Society Conference**, taking place 18–21 October 2026 in Perth.

These awards recognise outstanding contributions from students and early-career researchers and play a key role in supporting the next generation of lipid scientists.
